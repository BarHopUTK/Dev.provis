Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about implementing ProTrack

How long does installation take?

Typical installation takes 2-4 weeks depending on airport size and complexity. We work around your operational schedule to minimize disruption.

What's included in the warranty?

Our warranty covers all hardware components, software updates, and technical support. Extended warranty options are available for additional peace of mind.

Can the system integrate with existing ATC?

Yes, ProTrack is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing air traffic control systems. Our team handles all integration requirements during installation.

What maintenance is required?

Minimal maintenance is required. Our system includes remote monitoring and automated diagnostics. Annual on-site inspections are included in all packages.

How accurate is the detection system?

ProTrack achieves sub-meter accuracy within 2km range with position updates every 100ms. False positive rates are less than 1%.

What training is provided?

Comprehensive training is included for all operational staff. We provide both classroom and hands-on training, plus ongoing support as needed.
